What We Do

Our Psychosocial Rehabilitation Program serves individuals with a primary diagnosis of mental illness. Our program is based on promoting and practicing the following principles of recovery.

  • Learning Skills
  • Self-determination
  • Natural & Community Supports
  • Individualized Intervention
  • Early Intervention
  • Caring Environment
  • Dignity and Respect
  • Consumer Choices
  • Functioning & Support in Real World Environments

What We Offer

Wellness Recovery

Helping participants function in environments of their choice. Services are provided at the CCDA facility and in the community.

Expansive Facilities

Our 12 acre facility includes a large educational multi-room facility, a recreation center, vegetable garden, goat habitat, and a variety of indoor and outdoor activities.

Skill Development

We provide education and prevocational activities such as community living, personal care, social relationships, and symptom management based on medical necessity.

Our Philosophy

Everyone has the potential to grow, Change and recover. Self-determination happens when people are respected and supported in their own choices.
